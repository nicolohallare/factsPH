Source Guide
Admin / Bagong Pilipinas
PNA · Manila Bulletin
Pro-Marcos administration framing. State newswire and admin-leaning outlets that emphasize government achievements and official positions.
DDS / Pro-Duterte
Manila Times · Manila Standard
Frame Duterte positively. Critical of ICC and Marcos admin when it conflicts with Duterte interests. Supportive of Sara Duterte.
Mainstream / Centrist
GMA News · Inquirer · Philippine Star · ABS-CBN
Legacy outlets. Report across the spectrum with varying editorial lean shaped by ownership and advertisers.
Progressives / Pink / Civil Society
Rappler · Vera Files · Kodao
Critical of Duterte-era impunity. Pro-ICC, pro-accountability, human rights and liberal opposition aligned.
⚠️ These labels describe editorial tendencies, not absolute truth. Every outlet has biases shaped by ownership and history. Read across the spectrum.
About FactsPH
🇵🇭

FactsPH is a free, independent news reader built for Filipinos who want to understand the full picture of Philippine politics — not just one side of it.

We pull live headlines from across the political spectrum — from admin-aligned outlets to Duterte-leaning broadsheets to progressive digital media — and use AI to summarize each story from all perspectives.

How it works

📰 Live RSS feeds are fetched from 9+ Philippine news outlets every time you refresh. Stories covering the same topic are clustered together, then AI generates a neutral summary plus each outlet's distinct political framing.

Why it exists

Philippine media is deeply polarized. Reading only one outlet gives you only one version of reality. FactsPH shows you all versions side by side — so you can think for yourself.

Built with

Claude AI (Anthropic) · Open-source RSS feeds · Vercel · No ads, no trackers, no login required.

FactsPH is not affiliated with any political party, outlet, or government body. AI summaries may contain errors — always read the original source.
